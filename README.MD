# 🌍 Urban Heat Mitigation AI

An AI-powered geospatial analysis project that identifies urban heat hotspots, analyzes the factors contributing to urban heating, and recommends effective cooling strategies using satellite imagery, OpenStreetMap data, and Machine Learning.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Urban areas experience higher temperatures than surrounding rural regions due to the **Urban Heat Island (UHI)** effect. This project aims to:

* Identify urban heat hotspots.
* Analyze environmental factors influencing heat.
* Generate heat risk maps.
* Recommend cooling strategies for urban planners.
* Build a Machine Learning model for heat risk prediction.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Collect and preprocess geospatial datasets.
* Analyze Land Surface Temperature (LST).
* Study vegetation using NDVI.
* Analyze buildings, roads, and water bodies.
* Generate a Heat Risk Index.
* Train an AI/ML model to predict urban heat intensity.
* Visualize results through maps and dashboards.
